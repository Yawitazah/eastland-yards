Single-Family & Townhomes from the high $400k’s

Charlotte natives will surely remember Eastland Mall, which opened on Central Avenue in the mid 70’s and at the time was the state’s largest mall.

Developer Crosland Southeast is overseeing the redevelopment of the 69-acre site, now called Eastland Yards, with plans to include residential, office, retail, hotel, sports and/or community facilities.

The residential section of Eastland Yards will include apartments, single-family homes and townhomes; Saussy Burbank has been named the exclusive builder of the latter two.

Location

Located in East Charlotte, Eastland Yards is convenient to Plaza Midwood, highways 24/27, I-77, I-85, I-277 & 74, and Uptown Charlotte.

Eastland Park

The future 4.5-acre Eastland Park, designed by Mecklenburg County Parks and Recreation will include two lawn areas (one artificial and one natural turf with terraced seating), walking trails, covered picnic and performance areas, a restroom, playground with hillside play, splash pad, a multi-purpose court area (that can accommodate basketball, futsal, and hockey), skateable elements, fenced dog lounge, seating and landscaping.
